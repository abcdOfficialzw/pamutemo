# PaMutemo — App Store Copy

## Promotional Text
Know the correct traffic fine, protect yourself at roadblocks, and stay ready before you drive. PaMutemo puts Zimbabwe’s official deposit‑fine schedule in your pocket—fast, offline, and easy to search.

## Description
PaMutemo helps Zimbabwean drivers check the official deposit fines for traffic offences quickly and confidently.

Key features:
- Fast search across offences, sections, and categories
- Offline access to the fines schedule (no data required once installed)
- Roadblock Mode to show the offence and amount clearly
- “Am I Ready?” checklist to avoid common roadblock penalties
- Know Your Rights tips for calm, respectful interactions

Why PaMutemo:
- Clear, lightweight, and built for real‑world roadblock situations
- Designed for speed and low data usage
- Simple, focused experience—no clutter

Note: Fine amounts are based on the official deposit‑fine schedule and may change when new statutory instruments are issued.
